Persona

The persona definitions are based on the different users of the board in the company

Developer

Needs to see the status of the environment he is about to deploy to if someone else is using it. In case there is an issue, he needs to know who was the last one to deploy. Occasionally he needs to know if tests run OK

QA Engineer

Will use the board to see the status of tests, if they are running, what are the results, in which environment and on what browser there are bugs. It is alse important to know how many threads have failed

Project Manager

The Project Manager wants the developers to use the board in order to raise awareness to their open tickets. She will use the board herself to monitor the number and age of open tickets and who they are assigned to.
