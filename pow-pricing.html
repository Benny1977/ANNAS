Powtoon Pricing Page

Powtoon had a few different subscription plans - pay per use, monthly subscription, 6 months subscription and annual plan. The page did not convert well.
My task was to redesign the page so it will bring more conversions.

Jump to Design
My Work

Interviewing stakeholders in the company to understand business need.
Creating a new information hierarchy to make the existing plans clearer.
Consulting CMO on reducing plans.
Final Design of the page.

Design

The solution was separating the pay per use plans from the subscription plans in two tabs.
The subscription tab was selected by default as it had more business value, but the pay per use go tab needed to be visible.
In order to make the pay as you go tab still noticeable, I moved it to the left.

Subscription Tab

The best plan was marked. The 6 Months subscription was removed in order not to overload the page.

Pay Per Use Tab

Due to the location on the right, the tab was clicked many times, though it wasn't selected
