Samanage Mobile App

The mobile app is designated for technicians and it allows them to perform most common tasks while they are away from their desktop computer
For instace, when they are at a client's desk solving an issue and they want to see more details, open a request for them or get the latest notification update

Design

Some examples of the final design

Login Screen

Incidents Index

The Incidents index is the default screen that is displayed after logging in to the application, as it is the most important screen in the app

Main Menu and Notifications

New notifications appear in push, but there is also an indication on main menu level
The notifications screen is divided into 3 groups by notification date - today, yesterday and older
When there are no notifications from yesterday and today, the "older" title doesn't appear

Incident View

The view screen is divided into 3 tabs, to make it easier to get to the needed information
The main screen is Info and it shows the requester, title, description, state, attachments and comments
It also allow to comment and add attachments and shows SLA breach information that can be important

Service Catalog

The Service Catalog consists of two levels - category and subcategory
Clicking on each item opens a request form
