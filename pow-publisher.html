Powtoon Publisher

Powtoon allows you to create animated presentations and publish them as videos. Most of the users chose to publish their creations of YouTube.
The main purpose of the publisher is to encourage users to publish their videos on the Powtoon website.
Still keeping the YouTube option available, as many use it

Persona

The top users of Powtoon and their publishing habits

Margaret

School Teacher

Margaret uses Powtoon to create presentations for her class, as she thinks it's a great way to engage her classroom. She doesn't have much exports - only the presentations she needs for her lessons. She would export to Powtoon website if it is cheaper.

Rachel

Marketing Manager

Rachel uses Powtoon to prepare presentations when she wants to impress her audience, prefering it to PowerPoint. She makes an export a week, depends on the meetings she has. She cares much about the final quality of her exports. She would export to Powtoon website if it is cheaper.

Ben

Student

Ben encounterd Powtoon in school and started using it for school assignments. Later on he started using PowToon for fun - to make funny videos and share them on his blog. He makes few exports a week. He prefers exporting to YouTube, so he can embed the link in his blog
