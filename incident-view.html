Incidents View

The Incident view page is focused on a specific incidet and shows all the information that is related to it -
such as description, comments, tasks, similar incidents, suggested solutions etc.
The main guideline in redesigning the view page was to create a good information hierarchy,
so that the most important information and most common actions would be at the front

Wireframes

The solution was to divide the screen in few parts - main content, main attributes and widgets panel
The main attributes panel was divided to tabs when the comments tab is in the front, as commenting is essential to the incident flow
The top of the screen was meant to be dedicated to the information about the requester, to bring forward the user in the middle approach
However, it created some design issues, competing with the page title, so we decided to go for a different solution
