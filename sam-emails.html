Samanage Emails

Many Samanage clients prefer to get notifications about work progress by email, without logging in to the application. Some of them don't even have users.
Therefore the emails need to be informative and contain all the information that might be needed.
The challange was creating a ballance between a large amount of data and the focus of the specific email notification.

Design

The solution was creating distinct sections in the email with clear hierarchy.
The main section was the reason the specific email was sent, it was located on top of the email and had more prominent font
Other sections, such as Recent Activity, Incident Details and Attributes appeared below in chronological order
