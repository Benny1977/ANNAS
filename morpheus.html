Morpheus Platform

Morpheus is an online platform that enables users to recreate a jewelry item based on a photo with the aid of a 3D design professional.
The platform is a place of collaboration between the designer and the client. It allows the client to follow up on the status of his order, view the design and comment on it

Persona

The persona definitions are based on stories of existing clients

Jane

"Oh my god, where is my earring?!..."

Had recently lost one of her grandmothersâ€™ earrings. This earring had a great signifecance for her. She went to a consultation with a jeweler who offered a price that is to high for her. She heard about the website from a friend and is wiling to try it out. She doesn't know much about jewelry, only what the jeweler told her.

Robert

"My wife would love it..."

A saavy internet user who loves innovations. He doesn't know much about jewelry, but his wife loves it, so he has some experience buying jewelry to her. Recently he and his wife watched together the movie Gladiator on DVD, where she saw a wonderful necklace on one of the characters. Now with the date of their silver wedding approaching, Robert wants to surprise her with a recreation of this necklace.

Liz

"I want a ring just like yours..."

A frequent online shopper who loves jewelry and knows about different materials and jems. She is also familiar with jewelry price ranges. Recently one of her coworkers came back from her holiday wearing a very special ring she bought during the trip. She decided to recreate the same ring for herself and would look for a lower price.

Project Page

The project page is where the client can see all the project details and follow up.
