Samanage Feed

The Feed shows the full activity on a specific incident, including comments, tasks, updates, approvals etc. in a chronological manner.
It allows agents to follow up on latest activity on an incident and collaborate with each other.
One of the main collaboraion features is adding a subcomment on any feed item
