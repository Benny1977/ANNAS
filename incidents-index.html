Incidents Index

The most important and most viewed page in the Samanage application is the Incidents Index page. It is the list of all existing requests in the system.
The main usecases on this page are filtering and sorting, in order to scope the data that is shown.
Another common use case is quickly previewing an incident to understand what it is about without leaving the index
The task here was redesigning the experience and the look and feel

Persona

The main users and usecases of the index page

Call Center Rep

A junior and unspesialized technician, responsible to receive and reassign incoming new incidents. They would monitor incoming incidents in the general index. In many cases they would create new incidents in the system while on the phone with a client. Sometimes they would resolve simple issues.

Technician

Specialized in a specific area and receives only incidents in their area of expertise. Their work might take few days and they can work on several incidents in paralel. They would filter the index by incidents assigned to them or by category that is relevant to them

Service Desk Manager

The manager is usually a senior technician that is responsible to manage the service desk. They would apply different filters to the index to monitor the work and in order to create reports for their superiors. In some cases they would personally get involved in a specific incident.
